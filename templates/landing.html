Welcome

This is Repictionary, the opposite of pictionary, an AI Powered 2 player game. First, find yourself a partner.

Now, the AI is Trippy so expect funny things. The game is quite simple. Player 1 will write a description of an image for Player 2 to guess. Genius AI artist will paint the picture, and Player 2 will guess. Scored based on how close the answer is to the original sentence.

You will be directed to the game options page, after which you will begin the game.

Here's 2 example captions for images to give you an idea: (1) A potato salad in a bowl. (2) People on the beach
